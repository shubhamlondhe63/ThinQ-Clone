What is ThinQ?

The Indian Navy takes immense pride to announce their flagship event, a unique national level quiz competition – ‘THINQ’ . To celebrate ‘Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav’ and commemorate the glorious journey of 75 years of independence of our great nation, we invite 7,500 schools from across the country.

This event aims at providing the brightest, young minds, the future leaders of our country, a unique opportunity to experience the ‘way of life’ of the Indian Navy and instil a sense of national pride and patriotism whilst testing their knowledge and intellect.

The event which would begin with interesting quizzing rounds will turn into an experience of a lifetime for the top 16 teams (each consisting of two participants and one guide/teacher) who will get an unparalleled opportunity to participate in an all-sponsored trip for the semi-finals and the grand finale. Additionally, these lucky teams get the golden chance to battle their opponents at the prestigious cradle for future naval leadership – The Indian Naval Academy, Ezhimala where students will witness the ‘Passing out Parade’ of the naval cadets (https://www.ina.gov.in/ ) and visit the magnificent aircraft carrier INS Vikramaditya .

The qualifying teams get the chance to meet and interact with Naval Personnel in uniform and understand the exciting world of Naval Operations onboard warships, submarines and naval aircraft through experience sharing with personnel who have dedicated their lives to our nation.

The participation and achievements of all the students will be recognised and honoured by award of special appreciation certificate and several exciting prizes for this unique occasion.
